About Me

Raised in Kenora, Ontario, I have a love for business and a passion for computer science. I am focused on refining the necessary skills for success and gathering valuable experience in an effort to reach my dreams.

In the summer of 2022, I worked full-time at the Kenora & District Chamber of Commerce. I helped local businesses take advantage of the chamber's services through a proactive method that prioritized their satisfaction and success. After spending the summer around wise business professionals, I developed skills in leadership, collaboration, and initiative. In October of the same year, I parlayed this success into a rewarding role as a member of the Interact club, where I help raise money and awareness of important issues and causes.

Now, I possess the skills and knowledge that make me an asset to any employer in the business field. I have an outstanding work ethic that allows me to overcome challenges and adversity. I have leadership and collaboration skills that help others around me work at their full potential. And my passion fuels my desire for continued growth and continued success.
